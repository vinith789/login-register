Frequently Asked Questions

What are the mall opening hours?

We are open daily from 10 AM to 10 PM.

Do you provide home delivery?

Yes! Delivery is available for selected stores inside Fun Mall.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have ample parking space for cars and bikes.

Do you have a kids' play area?

Yes, we have a safe and fun kids' play zone with games and activities.

Are there wheelchair facilities?

Yes, we provide wheelchair access and ramps throughout the mall.

Do you host live events?

Every weekend, we host live shows, music events, and cultural programs.

Can I book event space at Fun Mall?

Yes, our event hall can be booked for private or corporate events. Contact our events team for details.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major debit/credit cards, UPI, wallets, and cash payments are accepted.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, Fun Mall gift cards are available at the customer care desk.

Are pets allowed inside the mall?

Pets are not allowed inside, except for certified service animals.
